The Royal's Story

For over 18 years, ROYAL'S LOGISTICS has been the undisputed leader in corporate employee transportation, delivering precision, safety, and royal comfort to Hyderabad's top IT giants, BPOs, and MNCs.

Founded in 2006 with just five vehicles and an unwavering commitment to punctuality, Royal's Logistics grew organically through word-of-mouth — earning every contract through flawless execution, not just promises. Our founder, K. Naga Kumar Raja, recognized early that corporate employees deserved more than a cab ride; they deserved a reliable, dignified, and safe commute that allowed them to begin and end their workday stress-free.

Today, we operate 100+ well-maintained, GPS-enabled vehicles, serve 10+ enterprise accounts across Hyderabad's IT corridors — from HITEC City to Gachibowli, Madhapur to Kondapur — and log over 2.3 million safe journeys. Our operations never stop; a dedicated 24/7 command center monitors every vehicle in real time, ensuring zero lapses in service delivery.

We don't just move employees — we orchestrate seamless mobility ecosystems. Our advanced route optimization algorithms, pre-trip safety checks, experienced driver corps (averaging 9+ years with Royal's), and dedicated women's safety protocols make us the gold standard in corporate transport across Telangana.

Our Journey Through Time

From 5 vehicles to 100+ — a legacy built on trust

2006 — THE FOUNDING
Royal's Logistics is Born

K. Naga Kumar Raja launches Royal's Logistics in Hyderabad with 5 vehicles and a bold mission: to deliver corporate transport with the reliability and dignity of a royal service. First client acquired within 30 days.

2009 — FIRST MAJOR IT CONTRACT
Entering the IT Corridor

Signed our first large-scale IT company contract, managing transportation for 100+ employees daily across HITEC City routes. This marked the beginning of our specialization in corporate employee transport.

2012 — FLEET EXPANSION
Growing to 30+ Vehicles

Fleet expanded to 30 vehicles including the introduction of SUVs and tempo travellers for larger employee batches. Established our first dedicated operations room with trained coordinators.

2015 — ISO CERTIFICATION
Quality Goes International

Achieved ISO 9001 Certification — formalizing our quality management processes, driver training frameworks, and vehicle maintenance standards that we had been practicing since inception.

2018 — TECHNOLOGY LEAP
Launching the Employee Tracking App

Introduced real-time GPS tracking app for employees, enabling live cab location, driver profile, ETA notifications, and SOS emergency feature — setting a new industry benchmark for transparency and safety.

2020 — 100+ FLEET MILESTONE
Century of Royal Vehicles

Fleet crossed 100 vehicles. Added luxury coaches and large 30–50 seater Volvo buses. Launched 24/7 command center with dedicated night-shift monitoring team for BPO clients.

2022 — SAFETY MILESTONE
3 Consecutive Zero-Incident Years

Achieved three consecutive years of zero major incidents — a testament to our defensive driving training, rigorous vehicle checks, and proactive safety culture embedded across the entire fleet.

2024 — INDUSTRY LEADERSHIP
Hyderabad's Most Trusted Transport Partner

Serving 10+ Fortune 500 and enterprise clients, 2.3M+ journeys completed, ISO 9001:2024 renewed, and expanding operations to new IT clusters. Royal's Logistics stands as Hyderabad's undisputed corporate transport leader.
