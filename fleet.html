Royal Maintenance Standards

Every vehicle undergoes our 5-Star Imperial Maintenance Protocol


Weekly Service

Every vehicle undergoes a comprehensive 50-point mechanical inspection every two weeks — engine, brakes, tyres, electrical, and A/C systems are checked by our certified in-house mechanics before any vehicle is cleared for operation.

Weekly Sanitization

All interiors are deep-cleaned using hospital-grade disinfectants every week. Seat covers are laundered, AC filters are cleaned, and upholstery is vacuumed — ensuring a fresh, hygienic environment for every employee every day.


GPS Calibration

All GPS units are calibrated monthly to ensure real-time tracking accuracy. Our command center receives live feeds from every active vehicle, enabling precise sub-minute ETA calculations and instant deviation alerts.

Daily Pre-Trip Checks

Every morning, drivers run a mandatory pre-trip inspection checklist — verifying fuel levels, tyre pressure, brake response, lights, and GPS before the first pickup. Only cleared vehicles are deployed on corporate routes.

Insurance & Compliance

Every vehicle carries comprehensive insurance, valid road tax, PUC certification, fitness certificates, and all mandatory transport permits from the Telangana Transport Department. Zero tolerance for non-compliance.

Emergency Backup Fleet

In the rare event of a breakdown, our rapid-response backup fleet deploys a replacement vehicle within 30 minutes to the exact location. The 24/7 operations center manages all emergency responses in real time.
