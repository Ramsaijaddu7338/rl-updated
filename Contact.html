Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our corporate clients

What locations does Royal’s Logistics cover in Hyderabad?
We cover all major IT hubs including HITEC City, Gachibowli, Madhapur, Financial District, Kondapur, and surrounding residential areas.
How quickly can services be started?
Services can be activated within 48–72 hours after onboarding. Large deployments may require 5–7 days for smooth execution.
What safety measures are implemented?
We ensure verified drivers, GPS tracking, panic alerts, and strict safety protocols including priority drop for women employees.
What happens during breakdowns?
Backup vehicles and drivers are available 24/7. Immediate replacements are dispatched to avoid delays.
Do you provide reports for HR teams?
Yes, we provide detailed reports including trip summaries, punctuality metrics, and performance insights.
Are vehicles GPS tracked and maintained?
All vehicles are GPS-enabled, sanitized regularly, and maintained with strict servicing schedules.
