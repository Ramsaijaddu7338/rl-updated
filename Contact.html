Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our corporate clients

What areas in Hyderabad does Royal's Logistics cover?
We cover all major IT and corporate corridors — HITEC City, Gachibowli, Madhapur, Nanakramguda, Financial District, Kondapur, Kukatpally, Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Secunderabad, and all surrounding residential zones.
How quickly can Royal's Logistics start services for a new client?
Once a contract is signed and employee data is shared, our operations team can deploy a fully functional transport system within 48–72 hours. For large deployments (100+ employees), we recommend a 5–7 day onboarding period.
What safety measures are in place for women employees during night shifts?
Police-verified drivers only, female escort guards on request, panic button integration in all vehicles, real-time tracking accessible to family members, first-in first-out drop policy for women, and immediate escalation protocols.
What happens if a cab breaks down or a driver doesn't show up?
We maintain a dedicated backup fleet on 24/7 standby. Any breakdown or no-show is resolved within 15 minutes — a replacement vehicle and driver are dispatched immediately via our command center.
Can we get customized reports for our HR team?
Absolutely. Every client receives monthly HR-ready dashboards with trip summaries, punctuality statistics, driver performance scorecards, route compliance reports, and cost optimization insights.
Does Royal's Logistics provide vehicles for corporate events or one-time bookings?
Yes. From executive sedans for VIP guests to 50-seater luxury buses for conferences and team outings. Pricing is based on vehicle type, distance, and duration. Contact us for a customized quote.
Are all vehicles GPS-tracked and sanitized?
Yes, without exception. Every vehicle is GPS-enabled and tracked live. All vehicles are professionally sanitized before every shift. Bi-weekly mechanical servicing ensures zero breakdowns on the road.
How do I get a quote or proposal for my company?
Fill out the contact form above, call +91 9949900008, or WhatsApp us. Our team will prepare a detailed, customized proposal within 2 business hours — at absolutely no obligation.
