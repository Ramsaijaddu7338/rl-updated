Royal Operation Process

From onboarding to daily commute — a flawless 5-step system managed by our 10+ year veterans

01
Consult & Map Routes

Our operations team meets with your HR/Admin to understand workforce size, shift timings, pickup zones, and special requirements. We conduct a thorough geospatial analysis to design the most efficient, employee-friendly route network. Every route is validated against live traffic patterns before finalizing.

02
Driver Allocation

Experienced, background-verified drivers are assigned to specific routes based on locality familiarity, shift compatibility, and skill level. Night shift routes always receive our most senior drivers. Driver-employee continuity is maintained wherever possible for added comfort and trust.

03
Fleet Inspection

Before every shift, each vehicle undergoes a mandatory pre-trip inspection: tyres, brakes, AC, GPS device, fuel, sanitization status, and safety kit (first aid, fire extinguisher). Any vehicle that fails inspection is immediately replaced with a standby vehicle — zero compromise on quality.

04
Live Monitoring

Once vehicles are deployed, our 24/7 command center tracks every cab in real time via GPS. Alerts are triggered for route deviations, speeding, emergency SOS activations, or unexpected delays. Coordinators proactively communicate with drivers and employees to resolve any issues within minutes.

05
Feedback & Improvement

After each cycle, employees and HR teams can submit feedback via the app or dedicated helpline. Our operations team reviews all feedback weekly, identifies improvement areas, and implements changes within the same week. This continuous improvement loop is what keeps our on-time performance at 99.8%.

Technology Powering the Royal Experience

Innovation that keeps your employees safe, on time, and informed

Real-Time GPS Tracking

All 100+ vehicles are equipped with high-accuracy GPS units transmitting location data every 10 seconds. Our command center displays live fleet positions, enabling instant response to any deviation or emergency. Employees can view their cab's live location from the moment it's dispatched.

AI Route Optimization

Our intelligent routing algorithm processes thousands of variables — traffic density, road closures, pickup point clusters, employee shift times, and vehicle capacity — to generate the most time-efficient, cost-effective routes. Routes are recalculated in real time whenever conditions change.

HR Analytics Dashboard

Corporate HR teams get access to a comprehensive dashboard with daily trip reports, attendance tracking, on-time statistics, cost per trip breakdown, driver performance ratings, and monthly summary analytics — giving complete operational visibility and data-driven decision support.
