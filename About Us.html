Who We Are

ROYAL'S LOGISTICS is Hyderabad's most trusted corporate employee transportation company — built on a foundation of 18+ years of service excellence, safety-first operations, and an unwavering commitment to punctuality.

Founded in 2006 by Mr. K Naga Kumar Raja, we started with a simple but powerful vision: "Every employee deserves a commute that is safe, punctual, and dignified — that's the royal standard." From 8 vehicles and 2 clients, we have grown into a 100+ vehicle Our Fleet serving 10+ of Hyderabad's top IT companies, BPOs, and MNCs.

Today, Royal's Logistics is synonymous with reliability, professionalism, and care in corporate transport. Our 2.3 million+ safe journeys, zero major incident record (5+ years), and 98.7% client retention rate speak for themselves. We are not just a transport vendor — we are a long-term mobility partner for the organizations we serve.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that drive every decision at Royal's Logistics

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and dignified corporate employee transportation that empowers organizations to focus on their core business — while we take complete care of their workforce mobility needs, 24/7, 365 days a year.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted corporate employee transport brand — recognized for operational excellence, safety leadership, and for setting the gold standard in employee commute experiences across every city we serve.

Royal Standard

We hold every driver, vehicle, and operation to the "Royal Standard" — a benchmark we defined: zero delay, zero compromise on safety, maximum comfort, and absolute professionalism in every single trip.

Safety First

Safety is not a policy at Royal's Logistics — it is our culture. From panic button integration to verified driver protocols, women safety escorts, and pre-trip inspections, safety is embedded in every operational layer.

Continuous Excellence
